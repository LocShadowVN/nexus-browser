Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nexus a replacement for Chrome or Brave?

Nexus is designed to be an ultra-lightweight, privacy-focused alternative. While it handles everyday browsing (reading, searching, watching videos) perfectly, it uses the OS's native WebView. Some highly complex web apps (like advanced Google Docs features) might not work as smoothly as they do on Chromium.

How does the AI integration work?

Nexus allows you to connect your own API keys for models like Claude, Qwen, or GLM. Your prompts are sent directly from your browser to the respective API endpoint. We don't route your data through any middleman servers.

Do I need to install Tor or WARP separately?

Yes. Nexus has 1-click toggle support for Tor and Cloudflare WARP, but you need to have their official clients installed and running in the background on your machine for the proxy connection to work.
