Core Features

Military-Grade Security

AES-256-GCM encryption & Argon2id hashing. Blocks trackers, ads, and spoofs browser fingerprints right out of the box.

Native AI Assistant

Powered by Claude, Qwen, and GLM 5.2. Your AI copilot is always available directly in the toolbar.

Extreme Performance

Written in Rust, using minimal RAM. Auto-freezes inactive tabs and downloads files with 16 concurrent threads.

Anonymity Layer

1-click integration for Tor Network and Cloudflare WARP. Enforces HTTPS and strips tracking parameters from URLs.

Productivity Tools

Built-in bookmarks bar, local history search, and session restore. Never lose your tabs again.

No Telemetry

Zero data collection. What you browse stays on your machine. 100% transparent and auditable code.

100% Open Source

Transparency is at the core of Nexus. There is no hidden telemetry, no secret data collection, and no closed-source black boxes. The entire engine is written in Rust and is available for anyone to inspect, modify, and build upon. Join us in making the web a safer and more private place.
